# Street-Art-Reporting-System

Street Art, Graffiti Report System is a PHP web-based application. 

Users under 3 categories: Artist, Evaluator and the Spectator. 

User categories of Artist & Evaluator are allowed to create their User 
Accounts and Sign Up on the System.
Logged In Artists are allowed to make report of a Street Art and (or)
Graffiti. Artists could update or delete their initial Report(s). 
Artists & Spectators could view reported Street Art, Graffiti only when it
gets registered by Evaluator. While the Evaluator has rights to deny registration.  
Evaluator make decision where to place reported Street Art, Graffiti and there 
are three plausible options: "private" (visible to Evaluator only), "protected"
(visible to Artist and Evaluator only) and "public" (visible to all three 
User categories).
Evaluator place Damage e.Sticker when Street Art does not meet requirements
of the Street Art. This Sticker must be provided alongside with an explanation.
Evaluator, Artist & Spectator should be able to use system only by 
accepting Terms of Service. This document must include statements, e.g. 
Artist reports must include information about consent from object owner
before making his Street Art. Without consent made Graffiti can't be 
treated as Street Art.
Evaluator & Artist Users must be adults. Artists may create their family Account.

Statistical data graphs on the Analysis page are available.
